About

Sean Herrera is an aspiring Web Developer, particpating in the New York TechImpact program at Laguardia Community College, and studying at the General Assembly of New York. He is the third child to an Irish mother and a Colombian father, and the proud owner of a Shih Tzu and Dachshund.

Hobbies

A healthy mind is an active mind, and to this end, Sean has several hobbies to keep his mind in motion.

Recycled Computation

More a hobby of circumstance, Sean enjoys salvaging computers that have been left out on garbage day, and building computers from his growing stockpile of functional components. He's particularly fond of hardware from the late 90's and early 2000's.
